Settings

Configure how VEIL launches, behaves, and themes itself.

Startup

Run the VEIL backend automatically when you sign in to Windows.

Run at startup

Backend is launched headlessly; the UI window stays hidden until you open it.

Theme

VEIL ships with a dark glass theme inspired by Mica, Liquid Glass, and Arch.

Data pull rates

How often VEIL polls system data. Lower numbers mean more responsive widgets but slightly higher CPU use.

Reset

Clear cached registry data and restart the backend. Your installed addons stay put.
